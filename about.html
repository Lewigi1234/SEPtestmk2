Welcome to SEPtest, a cybersecurity consulting firm focused on protecting businesses from social engineering attacks. Our mission is to help organizations build a robust security posture to defend against ever-evolving threats. By working with us, you will be provided with excellent service from an industry professional who is passionate about Cyber Security and it's importance in the modern world.

Past experience

...

Lewis always had a passion for cyber security from a young age, at 15 years old he was running pentests on his home network. He originally obtained a Bachelors degree in Electronic and Communications engineering. Then he diverted course and moved to London where he also found a passion for sales and people skills. After 2 years experience in the sales sector he decided to continue study with a Masters degree in Cyber Security at the University of Newcastle, UK, where he obtained the highest level of degree with distinction. Afterwards he secured a job working for a defence company called Leonardo where he was trained according to their specifications & obtained a CISSP qualification in the process.
