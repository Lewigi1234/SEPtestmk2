Our Services

At SEPtest, we offer a comprehensive range of social engineering penetration testing services to help organizations identify and mitigate potential security risks:

Phishing Assessment

We simulate phishing attacks to evaluate your employees' susceptibility to email-based threats and recommend countermeasures to strengthen your organization's email security.

Vishing Assessment

Our team conducts targeted phone-based attacks to assess your employees' vulnerability to voice phishing (vishing) and helps you implement effective security measures to prevent unauthorized information disclosure.

Pretexting Assessment

Our experts use a variety of techniques to gather information from your employees and assess their vulnerability to pretexting attacks, helping you implement safeguards to protect sensitive data.

OSINT Research

We perform open-source intelligence (OSINT) research to gather publicly available information about your organization and its employees, identifying potential risks and vulnerabilities that could be exploited by attackers.

Physical Security Assessment

We conduct on-site evaluations to assess your organization's physical security controls and identify areas of improvement to protect against unauthorized access and theft.

Security Awareness Training

Our customized security awareness training programs are designed to educate your employees on the latest threats and best practices in cybersecurity, helping to reduce the risk of social engineering attacks.

Incident Response Planning

We help you develop and implement an effective incident response plan to mitigate the impact of potential security breaches and ensure a swift recovery.

Our team of experienced professionals is committed to helping you safeguard your organization from social engineering attacks and build a robust security posture. Contact us today to learn more about our services and how we can help you stay one step ahead of cyber threats.
