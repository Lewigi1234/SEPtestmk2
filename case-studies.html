Case Studies

Case Study 1: Phishing Awareness Training

A mid-sized financial organization needed help to improve their employees' phishing awareness. We conducted a series of simulated phishing attacks, followed by targeted training sessions. The results showed a significant improvement in phishing detection and reporting rates among employees.

Case Study 2: Vishing and Pretexting Defense

A technology company was concerned about potential vishing and pretexting attacks. We designed a custom security awareness program, focusing on these specific social engineering techniques. The training increased employee vigilance and reduced the risk of successful attacks.

Case Study 3: OSINT Analysis and Countermeasures

A manufacturing firm wanted to assess their exposure to OSINT-based attacks. We performed a comprehensive OSINT analysis, identifying sensitive information that could be used in social engineering attacks. We then provided recommendations for securing their digital footprint and enhancing their overall security posture.
